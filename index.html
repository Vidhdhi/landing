Car Rental Coverage Areas in Northern Province

Ceylon Explore proudly serves the entire Northern Province of Sri Lanka with our comprehensive car rental services. Our primary base is in Jaffna, but we extend our services to all major towns and tourist destinations in the region:

Jaffna District: Jaffna city, Chavakachcheri, Point Pedro, Karainagar, Velanai, Kayts, and surrounding areas. Perfect for exploring the historic Jaffna Fort, Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, and beautiful northern beaches.

Kilinochchi District: Reliable transportation for visiting war memorials, archaeological sites, and agricultural regions. Our vehicles are perfect for both urban and rural exploration.

Mannar District: Specialized vehicles for visiting Mannar Island, Adam's Bridge (Ram Setu), and the famous Baobab Tree. We provide both regular cars and 4WD vehicles for different terrains.

Mullaitivu District: Comprehensive coverage for beach destinations, lagoons, and historical sites. Our service extends to remote areas with proper planning and coordination.

Vavuniya District: Strategic location coverage for travelers heading south or exploring the transition zone between Northern and North Central provinces.
